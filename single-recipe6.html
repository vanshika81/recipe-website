Honey cake is a well known dessert made famous by the South Indian bakeries, especially Iyengar bakery. This super delicious honey cake is made from whole wheat flour (atta), unrefined cane sugar and uses less oil. Thus, this one is a bit healthier too as compared to its counterparts.
